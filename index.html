Helpful Info

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Starline Hotel located?

Starline Hotel is on Biringi Avenue, near DUWASA Roundabout, Dodoma 41000, Tanzania — about 3 minutes from Dodoma Airport (DOD).

Does the hotel include breakfast?

Yes. Complimentary buffet breakfast is served daily from 7:00 AM to 11:30 AM for all guests.

How can I book a room?

Book direct via WhatsApp, call +255 764 302 888, or email starlinehoteltz@gmail.com. Online booking is also available on Trip.com and other platforms.

Do you offer SGR station pickup?

Yes. We provide pickup and drop-off from Dodoma SGR Station. Message us on WhatsApp with your train arrival time to arrange pickup in advance.

What facilities are available?

97 air-conditioned rooms, restaurant, fitness center, conference hall, free high-speed WiFi, complimentary self-parking, and SGR station pickup service.

What currency are prices shown in?

All room rates on this website are shown in TZS (Tanzanian Shillings). Contact us for the latest rates and availability.
